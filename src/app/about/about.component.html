About traderinsideme.com
TraderInsideMe provides educational and fact-based content on topics such as Price Action Trading, Trading Plan, Trading Psychology, Risk Management, Current Affairs in the Stock Market, etc. Content on this blog is completely based on personal research and facts. Readers are encouraged to gain as much knowledge as possible from this blog and apply it to the real market taking into consideration the risk involved in the stock market. The clear intension of this blog is to spread awareness and share knowledge on financial market. This blog by no means provides tips or tricks for trading in the stock market. This blog encourages readers to learn and understand trading and trade only at their own risk. Readers are advised to trade with capital which they can afford to lose.
